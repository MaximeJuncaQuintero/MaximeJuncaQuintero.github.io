NXU Think Tank Report

This think tank report, co-written by myself, explores the societal, economic, and employment impacts of AI tools like Chat GPT. It delves into the nuances between AI and human intelligence, the expert use of Chat GPT, and its transformative potential across various sectors. The report also discusses the broader implications of AI on education, the labor market, and the concept of work in the context of potential industrial revolution acceleration.
