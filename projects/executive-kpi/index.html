Executive KPI Mailing Tool

Client: IP Firm

Person of Contact (POC): Monica Junca (IP Firm POC)

Meeting Frequency: Daily

Project Length: One week

Context

An IP firm faced an operational challenge due to a missed billing reminder. This project aimed to overhaul existing procedures to prevent such oversights.

Objective

The objective was to overhaul existing procedures to mitigate future risks by reorganizing process classifications, standardizing formats, and implementing a tool for detailed reporting and alerts.

Implementation

As Project Leader, I led the initiative to redesign administrative procedures and implement the Executive KPI Mailing Tool, initiating development and integration.

Method

My approach involved an operational assessment, development of the tool with standardization of processes, and integration with a monitoring strategy.

Result

The tool goal is to reduce administrative errors.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • Process Classification and Standardization: Reorganized and standardized procedures.
  • Executive KPI Mailing Tool: Automated email system for monitoring and reporting key metrics.
