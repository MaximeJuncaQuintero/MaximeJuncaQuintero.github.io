Executive KPI Mailing Tool

Client: IP Firm

Meeting Frequency: Daily

Project Length: One week

Context

An IP firm confronted a critical operational challenge following a loss incurred due to a client's bankruptcy. A deeper investigation revealed that the root cause was a missed billing reminder, which had allowed the situation to escalate unnoticed. This incident highlighted several inefficiencies within the administrative processes. The administration's oversight was symptomatic of broader issues, including a complex organization of our process library, outdated classifications, a lack of distinction between recurring and non-recurring processes, varied formats, and the absence of monitoring tools for procedure application.

Objective

My proposal aimed at overhauling our existing procedures to mitigate future risks. I advocated for reorganizing our process classifications, distinguishing procedures by recurrence, standardizing process formats, and implementing a tool for detailed reporting and alerts. These measures were intended to streamline administrative operations and enhance oversight, particularly for critical recurrent tasks like billing reminders.

Implementation

Drawing from my analytical skills and understanding of our operational needs, I led the initiative to update administrative procedures and prepare implementation of the Executive KPI Mailing Tool. My responsibilities included conducting a comprehensive assessment of the existing processes, proposing a restructured framework, and overseeing the integration of the new tool.

Method

Operational Assessment and Recommendations: Conducted an in-depth review of current administrative processes, identifying inefficiencies and proposing strategic enhancements.

Tool Development and Process Standardization: Collaborated with a technical expert to design and implement the Executive KPI Mailing Tool, focusing on creating a uniform procedure format and automating performance reporting.

Integration and Monitoring Strategy: Ensured the seamless integration of the new tool with existing systems, establishing automated alerts for uncompleted tasks, and setting up a framework for regular oversight and adjustments.

Result

This foundation paves the way for future initiatives to further optimize operational efficiency and risk management, enhancing decision-making and allowing staff to focus on strategic growth-driving tasks.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • Process Classification and Standardization: Reorganized and standardized procedures.
  • Executive KPI Mailing Tool: Automated email system for monitoring and reporting key metrics.
