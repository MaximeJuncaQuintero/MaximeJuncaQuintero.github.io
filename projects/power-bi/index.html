Impact of Product Categories on Business Performance Metrics – POWER BI Project

This project analyzes the impact of different product categories (Office Supplies, Furniture, Technology) on business performance metrics including sales revenue, order quantity, and profitability, covering data from 2014 to 2017.

Context

This analysis explores consumer buying patterns and business strategies to understand how each product category affects overall business performance.

Objective

To provide a detailed analysis of the correlations between product categories and business performance indicators, identifying trends that could guide future business decisions and strategy formulations.

Method

Data was gathered from the "Business Analytics Dataset (2014-2017)" which includes comprehensive data on sales, customers, and product categories. Various analytical techniques were employed:

  • Analyzing sales trends across different product categories.
  • Evaluating order quantities to identify demand patterns.
  • Assessing profitability and discount strategies across categories.

Results

Key findings include Technology having higher revenues despite lower sales quantities, steady demand for Office Supplies with consistent profitability, and Furniture showing variable performance influenced by promotions and economic conditions.

Strategic insights suggest the need for tailored strategies for each category to maximize profitability and market penetration.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • Microsoft Power BI for data visualization
  • Python and SQL for data manipulation and analysis
