Amazon EU ATS KPI Library

POC: Richa Saxena (Manager), Sebastian Linares (Technical POCs)

Meeting Frequency: Once per week

Project Length: 3 months

Context

The project aimed to address the lack of a consolidated view of all EU ATS metrics and their update status in PerfectMile. Without a unified platform for tracking metrics, operational transparency and decision-making were hindered.

Objective

To develop an automated solution that synchronizes the local database (Redshift) with a PerfectMile Excel KPI file, providing a real-time, comprehensive view of metrics, their categories, affiliations to Business Reviews, and latest refresh statuses.

Implementation

As a Project Management Intern, I spearheaded the initiative, leveraging Excel macros and SQL queries to automate the data synchronization process.

Method

The execution involved analysis and planning, technical implementation, and exploring alternative solutions due to challenges in extracting ATS metrics directly from PerfectMile.

Result

The project adapted to technical challenges by enhancing metric visibility and monitoring through PerfectMile's existing features, like PerfectMetrics and Metric Audit, successfully addressing visibility and data consistency issues.

This project showcased my ability to navigate technical challenges, leverage analytical skills, and propose viable solutions in a complex business environment.

Tools and Technologies Used

  • Excel Macros
  • SQL Queries
